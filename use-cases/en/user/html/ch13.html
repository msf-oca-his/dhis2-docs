13.1. About indicators

Indicators are composed of multiple data elements, and typically consist of a numerator and denominator. Calculated totals do not have a denominator. You never enter indicators directly in DHIS 2, you derive them from combinations of data elements and factors. Indicators are used to calculate coverage rates, incidence and other values are a result of data element values that have been entered into the system.
